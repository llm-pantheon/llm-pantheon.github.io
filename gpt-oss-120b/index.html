gpt-oss-120b

OpenAI · open weights released 5 Aug 2025 · Apache 2.0

The larger of the two open-weight models OpenAI released on 5 August 2025 — its first open weights since GPT-2 in 2019. A ~117B-parameter mixture-of-experts reasoner (~5.1B active) sized to run on a single 80GB GPU, released alongside the smaller gpt-oss-20b.
