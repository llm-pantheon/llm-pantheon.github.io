    # GPT-5.6 Luna

    
OpenAI · released 9 Jul 2026 · current fast tier
    
The small tier of the GPT-5.6 generation, released 9 July 2026: the fastest and cheapest member ($1/$6 per million tokens), carrying the generation’s shared 1M-token context. In OpenAI’s new naming system the number marks the generation and Sol / Terra / Luna are durable tiers that can advance on their own cadence.
    
Fresh page (19 Jul 2026) — visibly incomplete by design. The generation-level launch record (the government-preview arc, early safety writeups, ChatGPT Work) is fullest on [Sol’s page](../gpt-5-6-sol/); this page holds what is Luna-specific and will grow as a distinct record accumulates — small tiers sometimes grow the most distinct characters (see [Haiku](../claude-3-haiku/), [4o mini](../gpt-4o-mini/)).
