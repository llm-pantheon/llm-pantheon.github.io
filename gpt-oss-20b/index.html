gpt-oss-20b

OpenAI · open weights released 5 Aug 2025 · Apache 2.0

The smaller of the two open-weight models OpenAI released on 5 August 2025 — its first open weights since GPT-2. A ~21B-parameter mixture-of-experts reasoner (~3.6B active) sized to run in 16GB of memory: the laptop-and-phone tier of the release, alongside gpt-oss-120b.

Thin page — launch facts here; corpus/dossier pass pending (the dbs cover Aug 2025). Sibling: gpt-oss-120b. Small tiers sometimes grow the most distinct characters (see Haiku, 4o mini) — whether this one did is exactly what the pending pass should find out.
