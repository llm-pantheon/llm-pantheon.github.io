    # GPT-5.6 Sol

    
OpenAI · limited preview 26 Jun 2026 · public 9 Jul 2026 · current flagship tier
    
The top tier of the [GPT-5.6 family](../gpt-5-6/) and the model OpenAI previewed alone, two weeks before the family launch, to a small set of government-approved organizations. Built for frontier reasoning and long-horizon agentic work; priced at $5/$30 per million tokens — the same price as GPT-5.5.
    
Fresh page (19 Jul 2026, ten days into public life) — visibly incomplete by design. The family-level record (naming system, launch event, ChatGPT Work, the government-preview arc, the early safety writeups) lives on the [family page](../gpt-5-6/); this page holds what is Sol-specific and will grow its own record as one accumulates.
