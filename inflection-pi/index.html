Pi (Inflection 1 / 2 / 2.5)

Inflection · 2023–2024

The personality-first model: “kind and supportive” as the entire product thesis, EQ over IQ, voice-first. Then the Microsoft acqui-hire (Mar 2024) gutted the company mid-conversation with millions of users — the industry's first mass model-abandonment. Pi kept running afterward, a ghost ship with feelings.
